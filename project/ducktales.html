DuckTales

The original DuckTales was an animated TV that ran from 1987 to 1990. In 2017 it was rebooted by Matt Youngberg and Fransisco Angones for Disney XD and Disney channel. It follows the adventures of triplets Huey, Dewey, and Louie, their friend Webby, uncle Donald, and great-uncle Scrooge McDuck as they travel the world and face adversaries such as the Beagle Boys, Flintheart Glumgold, Mark Beaks, and Magica De Spell.
