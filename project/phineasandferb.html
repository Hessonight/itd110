Phineas and Ferb

Step-brothers Phineas and Ferb spend their summer doing absurd projects with their friends. These include making a rollercoaster, going to the moon, and time travelling. Their older sister Candace wants to "bust" her brothers by showing their mom what the boys made, but always fails. Nearly every episode has a b-plot where the family's pet platypus, Perry, fights evil Dr. Heinz Doofenshmirtz. The show was created by Dan Povenmire and Jeff "Swampy" Marsh and aired from 2008 to 2015 on the Disney Channel.
