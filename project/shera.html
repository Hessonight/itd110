She-Ra and the Princesses of Power

Developed by Noelle Stevenson, She-Ra and the Princesses of Power is a reboot of the 1985 cartoon She-Ra: Princess of Power that premiered in 2018. Teenager Adora becomes She-Ra, a powerful princess who is tasked with defending Etheria from the Horde, who she was raised by. She makes friends with Glimmer, Bow, and the other princesses, while trying to mend her relationship with best-friend turned rival Catra. The series can be watched on Netflix.
