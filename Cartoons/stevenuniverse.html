Steven Universe

Created by Rebecca Sugar, Steven Universe has been airing on Cartoon Network since 2013. Titular character Steven Universe lives with the Crystal Gems, magical aliens Garnet, Amethyst, and Pearl. Steven is half-Gem, half-human, and his mother Rose Quartz had been the leader of the Crystal Gems. He, the Gems, and his best friend Connie go on adventures and fight off Homeworld Gems who want to take over the planet.
