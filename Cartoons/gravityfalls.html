Gravity Falls

Gravity Falls was a show on Disney Channel and Disney XD created by Alex Hirsch. It ran for two seasons from 2012 to 2016. Twins Mabel and Dipper Pines must spend their summer in Gravity Falls, Oregon, and work at the Mystery Shack, run by their great-uncle (often called "Grunkle") Stan. Though it seems as if their summer will be boring, it soon becomes clear that something supernatural is taking place in the small town.
